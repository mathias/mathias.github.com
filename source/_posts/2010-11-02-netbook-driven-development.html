Recently, there was a blog post shared on Hacker News titled Laptop Driven Development by Bernard Schaefer. The author is using a 1st gen Macbook Air, which had very limited CPU speed, RAM, and hard drive speed compared to most developer machines. The author explains how he uses the Unix/bash job control commands to pause his editor to run unit tests, and then restart the editor, for example. The comments on Hacker News did not really agree with this approach, and so I thought I'd write a follow-up about this method and share my own development methods. I use a hackintosh Dell mini9 netbook, so I am also limited in similar ways to Bernard. (1.6ghz Atom CPU, 2Gb RAM, 32gig SSD)
